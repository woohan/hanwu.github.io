    • Research

    I am currently working as a PhD student in the Dependable Distributed Systems (DDS) group at Free University of Berlin, directed by Prof. Dr. Katinka Wolter.
    I am doing research on the fault tolerance strategies in real-time streaming data processing. My research interests include performance evaluation, queueing theory, stream processing and machine learning.
